Image Coordinates

This tool will let you get the (X,Y) coordinates of any point of any image. Simply paste the URL of your image, click the Swap Image button, and then hover over the image to see the coordinates. To save a particular coordinate, click on the image.

 

 

To use this functionality on your own website, visit the Get Image Coordinates With jQuery article in our Code blog.
